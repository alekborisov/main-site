Intro

This is the personal site of the artist, philosopher, and web3 enthusiast Alek Borisov. Here you can find links to my projects, digital graphics, and texts.

Home > Intro


The main direction of my artistic and philosophical interest is human ontology and culture in the context of new technologies.

The transformation of body and consciousness by mixing them with digital substance is reminiscent of the work of alchemists with a philosophical elixir. With that, the progress of science and technology affects not only the person himself, but also his habitat, forming the post-digital ecosystem of Earth and the Solar System.

At the same time, the vector of development of new territories is shifting to the space of imagination, which is expressed in the creation and development of the Metaverse, brain-computer interfaces and neural network technologies.
